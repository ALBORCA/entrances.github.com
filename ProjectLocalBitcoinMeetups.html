Welcome to Local Bitcoin Meetups

Bitcoin meetups in every major town and soon also every small town globally and perhaps even universally!

URL: http://entrances.github.io/ProjectLocalBitcoinMeetups.html
Contact the admins of this list by sending a Bitmessage to: BM-2cTm9BgGNejSPVut4A7w2FhphqzMnVDeuT

You can network with other players in the emerging Bitcoin and cryptocurrency industries by meeting them locally and you can also buy and sell Bitcoin for fiat currencies.

There are other platforms available such as localbitcoins.com and meetup.com but the downside is that you cannot conveniently be anonymous on those websites. With Bitmessage, you can. Also Bitmessage uses encrypted technology making it one of the most secure ways of communicating possible.

Best practice:

You may want to switch to a private channel where you confirm each others identities or pseudoidentities if you find someone in the general chan that you want to meet up with.

Bear in mind that these channels are completely unmoderatable, for good and for worse. Defend yourself against the evil and approach what is good. Watch out for impostors, NSA agents, bankers and other suspicious elements. As long as most of the users are actually interested in meeting up to discuss Bitcoin and do business together this list should still be useful.

How to join:

Download Bitmessage from bitmessage.org

Go to File --> Join Chan
